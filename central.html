CENTRAL DELHI HOTELS

1. La Sagrita – A Boutique Hotel

La Sagrita is 3 km from New Delhi Railway Station and New Delhi Bus Station. The symbolic Red Fort is 7 km. The Indira Gandhi International Airport is 20 km away. Fitted with wooden flooring, each air-conditioned room is equipped with a heater and flat-screen satellite TV. The private bathroom includes shower. Some rooms have windows.

2. The Lalit New Delhi

The LaLit is a 10-minute drive from New Delhi Railway Station, while Indira Gandhi International Airport is 14 km away. The popular India Gate is 3.3 km away while the Red Fort is 4.3 km. Spacious, soundproofed rooms offer subtle touches of Indian culture. Generously furnished with a flat-screen TV and DVD player, some rooms offer an iPod dock. An electric kettle and a minibar are provided in each room. Iron and ironing board can be requested.

3. Vivanta Ambassador

Housed in a 1945 heritage building, Vivanta by Taj Ambassador is located in New Delhi, next to Khan Market. It offers a health club and spa. Free parking is provided. The historic sites of Lodhi Gardens and Humayan’s Tomb are less than 3 km from the hotel. Vivanta by Taj Ambassador is 22 km from Indira Gandhi International Airport. Thoughtfully planned rooms include little extras including Egyptian cotton towels and evening turndown service. Each room has a balcony and lounge area. Tea and coffee-making facilities and a safety deposit box are provided.

4. The Taj Mahal hotel New Delhi

Taj Mahal Hotel New Delhi is within 2 km from the well-known Rashtrapati Bhavan. Offering free parking, it is 18 km from the Delhi International Airport and 5 km New Delhi Railway Station. The Central Business District, the shopping area of Connaught Place and the business district of Gurgaon are within a 25-minute drive. Featuring modern Indian-style decor, luxurious rooms are equipped with fax machines and flat-screen cable TVs. They come with a safe and a sofa at the comfortable seating area. All rooms come with a view. .
